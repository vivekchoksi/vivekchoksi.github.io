Rise

Helping manage chronic conditions through exercise
A prototype of the app, built with Marvel

SUMMARY

Rise is designed to support patients with chronic conditions such as obesity and diabetes. Patients are referred by their doctors and placed into cohorts, small groups of patients who are managing the same illness. With Rise, patients can receive at-home treatment plans from their doctors, track their progress, and support other members of their cohort in achieving their goals.

My teammate and I built our rough prototype for Rise in 2015 as a five-week-long project for a Stanford d.school class on visual, interaction, and behavioral design. This class was my first introduction to digital design, and we used Sketch to create the graphics. Looking back on this project, I notice many design decisions I would make differently if I were to redesign the app with the skills I have now.
